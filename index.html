Movie Review

MazeRunner

MazeRunner is a 10/10 movie. It is about these teen boys who wake up in an open field surrounded by giant concrete walls. They are confused on what is going on and need to escape. There are doors that open each day and close each night and if you go outside them at night you are hunted by monsters. I would reccomend this movie for anyone who wants a thriller/suspence to help spice up their life.
